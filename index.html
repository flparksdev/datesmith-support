Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the DateSmith widget to my lock screen?

1. Long press on your lock screen
2. Tap "Customize"
3. Tap the widget area above the time
4. Find and select the DateSmith widget
5. Tap "Done"

Will the date update automatically at midnight?

Yes! DateSmith employs advanced background technology to ensure your date updates precisely at midnight, even when your phone is asleep.

Does DateSmith collect any personal data?

No. DateSmith is designed with your privacy in mind. We don't collect any data, require unnecessary permissions, or need an internet connection to function.

Will DateSmith drain my battery?

DateSmith is designed to be extremely battery efficient. Our background processes are optimized to use minimal resources while ensuring timely updates.

Are there any in-app purchases or subscriptions?

No. DateSmith is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions, no recurring fees, and no in-app purchases.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2025

DateSmith takes your privacy seriously. This policy describes what information we collect and how it is used.

Information Collection

DateSmith does not collect any personal information, analytics, or user data of any kind. The app functions entirely on your device and does not require internet access to operate.

Widget Data

DateSmith only accesses date and time information from your device's system to display the current date. This information never leaves your device and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

Background Processing

To ensure timely widget updates, DateSmith uses iOS background processing capabilities. This only involves system-level date information and does not access any personal data.

Third-Party Services

DateSmith does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or frameworks that would collect or transmit user data.
